Lodging

Full-service Cabins

The full-service cabins at Devil's Den State Parks have all the amenities guests need for a relaxing getaway. Although many of the cabins were built in the 1930s by the Civilian Conservation Corps, they are updated with modern amenities including satellite television, heat, and air conditioning, full kitchens with cookware and dinnerware. Linens are provided in these cabins. Outdoor seating is also provided. Cabins 2, 3, 5, and 7 are dog-friendly with added fees. Please call the park directly for details.

Camper Cabins

Camper Cabins are available as an option that is closer to the camping experience. These cabins sleep up to 4 people. There is no kitchen, bathroom, or television and guests must bring their own linens. Cabin guests share a family-style bathhouse which allows for full privacy in both bathrooms and showers. Each camper cabin has a screened porch with chairs, a dining table inside, and a picnic table, grill, and lantern hanger outside. These cabins have heat and air conditioning.

Additional Facilities

Large Pavilion with Restroom

Large Pavilion near Lee Creek and Devil's Den State Park day use area. Playground nearby. Picnic tables are included. Please contact the park to reserve this pavilion. Please contact the park at 479-761-3325 or devilsden@arkansas.com for reservations and details.

Price: $150/Day

CCC Pavilion with Restrooms

Built by the Civilian Conservation Corps in the 1930's and attached to the Ridge Runner Cafe, the CCC Pavilion is an iconic place for your gathering. Located near the pool and Devil Lake for your enjoyment. Please contact the park at 479-761-3325 or devilsden@arkansas.com for reservations and details.

Price: $55/Day

Visitor Center Meeting Room

A climate controlled room attached to the park visitor center, the meeting room is perfect for small gatherings, some audio-visual equipment available. Please contact the park at 479-761-3325 or devilsden@arkansas.com for reservations and details.

Price: $60/Day or $35/Use

Pool

The Pool is open seasonally (Memorial Day weekend to Labor Day weekend). Cabin guests receive free admission.
